About Swiss Diplomatic Plates

Swiss diplomatic plates are issued by the canton of Bern (BE) to vehicles of accredited foreign missions and international organisations. The plate format is:

BE · CD / CC / AT / SP / IO · Status # · Country / Org #

Corps Codes

CodeMeaning
CDCorps Diplomatique – bilateral diplomatic missions
CCCorps Consulaire – consular posts
ATPersonnel Administratif et Technique
SPPersonnel de Service
IOOrganisations Internationales

Country codes 1–181 and IO organisation codes are assigned by the Swiss Federal Department of Foreign Affairs (FDFA/DFAE).
